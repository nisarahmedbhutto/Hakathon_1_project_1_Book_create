# Introduction to PhysicalAI

Welcome to PhysicalAI for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This comprehensive resource provides cutting-edge technical content for robotics and AI engineers.

## What is This Resource?

This resource is a dynamic, comprehensive platform that evolves with the latest research, developments, and best practices in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Unlike traditional static textbooks, our content is constantly refreshed through AI agents that process research papers, documentation, and open-source repositories, with human review ensuring quality and accuracy.

## How It Works

Our system combines:
- **AI Agents**: Specialized agents that generate and update content based on the latest research
- **Human Review**: Expert validation to ensure technical accuracy and safety
- **Constitution-Guided**: All content follows our established ethical and scientific principles
- **Version Control**: Git-based tracking for all content changes and updates
